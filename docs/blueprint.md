# **App Name**: Sweet Surrender Bakery

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display high-quality images and descriptions of signature baked goods.
- Daily Specials: Highlight daily specials and promotions with visually appealing presentation.
- Contact Information: Provide clear contact information, including business hours and location map.
- Online Ordering CTA: Include a prominent call-to-action button for online ordering or menu viewing.
- Flavor Description Tool: An AI tool will reason about user reviews of your recipes, identify what customers say they like the most about your recipes, and incorporate those keywords and descriptive words when creating item descriptions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm brown (#A67B5B), evoking the rich colors of baked goods.
- Background color: Cream (#F5F5DC), providing a soft and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to add a touch of elegance and visual interest.
- Body font: 'Literata' serif for a literary, slightly formal feel. Use 'Literata' also for headlines.
- Use simple, elegant icons for navigation and categories, possibly hand-drawn style.
- Employ a grid-based layout with plenty of whitespace to create a clean and organized feel.
- Subtle hover effects on product images and buttons to add interactivity.
